What We Offer

Our Services

From boundary verification to full title deed processing — comprehensive land solutions backed by precision RTK GPS technology, experienced licensed surveyors, and complete legal compliance across all 47 counties of Kenya.

01 / 06

Land Subdivision

We subdivide land into legally compliant, properly registered plots tailored to your development goals. Our surveyors prepare Ministry of Lands-approved mutation documents and coordinate all county approvals for seamless registration.

02 / 06

Beacon Setting

Our licensed surveyors deploy RTK GPS equipment to set physical boundary beacons with millimetre precision. Every beacon is legally referenced to the national grid, giving you defensible, court-admissible boundary records.

03 / 06

Title Deed Processing

We manage the entire title deed lifecycle — from preparing survey plans and Land Control Board applications to lodging documents at the Lands Registry. We follow up at every stage so you receive your title deed fast and without hassle.

04 / 06

Land Transfer

We facilitate smooth, legally sound ownership transfers for both buyers and sellers. Our team handles stamp duty assessments, consent applications, Land Control Board hearings, and final deed registration — protecting your interest at every step.

05 / 06

GIS Mapping

We produce high-resolution GIS maps for land planning, environmental impact assessments, infrastructure development, and investment due diligence. All outputs are georeferenced to Kenya's national coordinate system and delivered in multiple formats.

06 / 06

Topographical Survey

Our topographical surveys capture detailed terrain data — contours, spot heights, natural features, and infrastructure — using total stations and drone photogrammetry. Essential for architects, engineers, and developers planning any construction or earthworks project.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a land subdivision take?

A straightforward subdivision typically takes 3–6 weeks from field survey to final title registration. Complex cases involving many plots or multiple county offices may take longer. We give you a clear timeline estimate at consultation.

What documents do I need to start?

You will need your original title deed or certificate of lease, a copy of your national ID or passport, KRA PIN certificate, and any existing survey plans. We review all documents at the consultation stage and advise on anything missing.

Do you operate outside Nairobi?

Yes — we operate across all 47 counties of Kenya. We have field teams in Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, Nyeri, and other regional centres. Remote counties can be served with appropriate advance notice.

How accurate are your GPS surveys?

Our Trimble RTK GPS systems achieve horizontal accuracy of ±10mm and vertical accuracy of ±20mm under clear-sky conditions. This exceeds the minimum accuracy standards set by the Survey Act of Kenya and the Institution of Surveyors of Kenya.

Can you help resolve a boundary dispute?

Yes. We conduct dispute surveys that produce certified boundary records admissible in court and at the Land Disputes Tribunal. Our reports clearly document measurements, methodology, and findings — giving you solid evidence to support your claim.
